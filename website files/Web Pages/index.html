Bear Island is an open world survival game. Build, explore, kill and survive in this challenging first person survival game.

Go To Download

World

Enter a living, breathing island, where every tree and plant can be chopped down and every animal killed.

Complete Player Freedom

Chop down trees to build a camp, or start a fire to keep warm. Scavenge food to keep yourself from starving. Craft a waterskin to collect water.

Build a small shelter or a large ocean side log cabin. Lay traps and defenses to keep a safe perimeter from the bears.

Gameplay

Explore and build during the day. Stay comfy in you base at night. Craft weapons and tools.

Bunker down during the evening or fend off the elements.

Fight or Hide

Use stealth to evade animals, or engage them directly with crude weapons built from sticks and stones.

A New Type of open world experience

Defend yourself against the constant attacking of bears. Play by yourself, or with up to 8 friends for the ultimate survival experience.
